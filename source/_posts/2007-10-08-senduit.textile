I love easy things. "Senduit":http://www.senduit.com does one thing; it enables you to create a private link to download a file. You control how long the link/file exists.

!http://scottwstatic.appspot.com/images/wlw/Senduit_13E9C/senduit_3.png(Senduit Image)!

This is a great way to quickly share files with friends, move a file to a remote computer, etc.
